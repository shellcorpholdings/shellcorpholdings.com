About ShellCorp

The institution
behind the brands.

ShellCorp Holdings Inc. is a Barbados-based digital holding company built to incubate, grow, and scale technology-driven ventures across sectors that define the modern Caribbean economy. We operate with the discipline of an institution and the conviction of a company that knows exactly what it's building.

Our portfolio spans eCommerce, AI strategy consulting, and real estate — three verticals with transformative growth potential across the Caribbean, Latin America, and beyond. Our mission is deliberate: to create interconnected digital infrastructure that keeps economic ownership rooted in the region.

"Born in the Caribbean.
Built for the world."

ShellCorp Holdings provides each subsidiary with governance, capital strategy, and operational infrastructure — while preserving the independence and identity that makes each brand exceptional. One parent. Three market leaders.

Shelley Shepherd
Founder & Chief Executive
ShellCorp Holdings Inc.
Shelley Shepherd is the Founder & CEO of ShellCorp Holdings Inc., a Barbados-based digital holding company building the Caribbean's next generation of technology-driven ventures. A trained AI consultant and fractional business strategist, Shelley partners with Caribbean businesses to unlock the transformative power of artificial intelligence — helping organisations that have long operated offline find their footing in a rapidly evolving digital economy. Driven by a belief that the Caribbean deserves world-class digital tools built by and for its own people, Shelley combines entrepreneurial ambition with strategic clarity — building ventures that are not just businesses, but pillars of a broader regional digital economy.
